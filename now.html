What I'm up to

Now

A snapshot of what's currently in my orbit — work, reading, places, thinking. Inspired by Derek Sivers' /now page idea: a living page, not a stale bio.

Last updated May 9, 2026 · edit

📍 Where I am

Tamil Nadu — currently somewhere between Chennai's Mylapore lanes and the bridge at Pamban. Slow mornings, long evening walks, and the occasional midnight basketball court.

🛠️ What I'm building

This blog itself, mostly. Slowly turning it into something I'd actually want to spend time on — quiet typography, careful photo presentation, a little ISS tracker on the side because why not. Side experiments live in scratch repos that may or may not see daylight.

📚 What I'm reading

Whatever's on the bedside table this week. Tends to be a mix of essays, the occasional novel, and a steady drip of long-form articles. Recently re-reading old posts on Slate Star Codex and bouncing between The Idea Factory and a stack of half-finished books I keep meaning to get back to.

📷 What I'm photographing

Temples, bridges, courtyards at the wrong hour. Anything with light that feels like it has a mood. The photos page has the latest few.

🤔 What I'm thinking about

The optimal rate of failure (more on the posts page). Why some places feel like they're holding a thought for you. Whether the best blogs are the ones that change slowly or the ones that change all the time. Slowly forming an opinion, slowly writing it down.

✋ Not doing

Twitter (mostly). Loud rooms. Long phone calls. Anything that doesn't survive a slow morning.
