Why I Started Blogging

April 27, 2026

Everyone has a story to tell. I wanted a place to put mine — somewhere between code and creativity, logic and emotion.

As developers, we spend so much time building things for others. But how often do we build something just for ourselves? This blog is that thing for me — a project with no deadline, no client, no requirements doc.

Writing forces you to think clearly. When you have to explain something in words, you discover what you truly understand and what you only think you understand. That alone makes it worthwhile.

"Writing is thinking. To write well is to think clearly."

I also believe in learning in public. Sharing what I'm working on, what I'm struggling with, and what I've figured out. Maybe something here will help someone else on their journey.

So here we are. No grand plan, no content calendar. Just me, writing when I have something to say. And that feels exactly right.
